What did we learn today?

Greek and Latin roots are super old, but they're still hiding in many words we use every day!
When you know these roots, you become a word detective. You can guess what big words mean, even if you've never seen them before!

Greek Root Words

Root Meaning Example
Auto Self automatic, automate, autobiography
Anti Against antibacterial, antidote, antithesis
Bio Life biography, biology, biodegradable
Geo Earth geography, geology, geometry
Graph Write autograph, graphic, demographic

Latin Root Words

Root Meaning Example
Circ Round circus, circulate
Dict Say dictate, edict, dictionary
Doc Teach document, docile, doctrinal
Fund Bottom Funding, fundamental
Jur Law Jury, jurisdiction
